# Yuri Lotman — Structure of the Artistic Text (1970)

## TL;DR / Abstract

Lotman gives the formal bridge between grammar and meaning. In *Structure of the Artistic Text* (1970), a literary text works as a dense sign system: rhythm, syntax, repetition, omission, and composition carry information, not decoration. That supports Lever 12: Russian brevity grammar changes meaning through form.

## Key points

No direct quotes here until a checked edition is added. This note anchors 3 claims:

1. Form is semantic, not ornamental.
2. A short fragment can carry more information than a full explanatory sentence.
3. Repetition and omission are structural signals.
